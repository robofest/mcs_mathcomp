2008 Annual Mathematics Competition Winners

The thirty-ninth annual Lawrence Technological University High School Mathematics Competition was held on Sunday, April 27, 2008. The competition is sponsored by the College of Arts and Sciences and the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at LTU, and is one of America's longest running high school mathematics competitions. Again this year, the exam was authored by Professor Michael Merscher of LTU. This year, over fifty of southeastern Michigan's brightest mathematics students competed for prizes of US savings bonds.
