2006 Annual Mathematics Competition Winners

The thirty-seventh annual Lawrence Technological University High School Mathematics Competition was held on Sunday, April 23, 2006. The competition is sponsored by the College of Arts and Sciences and the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at LTU, and is one of America's longest running high school mathematics competitions. Again this year, the exam was authored by Professor Michael Merscher of LTU. Over fifty of the Detroit area's finest high school mathematics scholars took the exam. Savings bonds are awarded to the top three finishers. The winners list is as follows:

2006
First Place Chaitanya Malla 12th grade Northville High School
Second Place Sunil Agarwal 11th grade Troy High School
Third Place Saurabh Pandey 11th grade Stoney Creek High School
