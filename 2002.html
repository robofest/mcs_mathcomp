College of Arts and Sciences
Department of Mathematics and Computer Science


Thirty-Third Annual
Mathematics Competition



Sunday, April 28, 2002

1:00 - 3:00 p.m.


Held at

Lawrence Technological University

    The Annual Mathematics Competition is open to all high school students. It is a feature of the Annual Lawrence Tech Open House Weekend, held this year on April 27 and 28. There is no fee for the competition and no prior registration is required. The test covers the mathematics commonly taught in high school up to but not including calculus. The First Place Award is a $200 savings bond, with Second Place awarded a $100 savings bond, and Third Place awarded a $50 savings bond. No calculators are allowed, and LTU will provide all test materials.
  • For more information, call the Mathematics and Computer Science Department at (248) 204-3560 or email Professor Michael Merscher at merscher@ltu.edu.
  • Lawrence Technological University is located at 21000 West Ten Mile Road, Southfield, Michigan. Open the map or call (248) 204-3560 for directions.
