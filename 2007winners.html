2007 Annual Mathematics Competition Winners

The thirty-seventh annual Lawrence Technological University High School Mathematics Competition was held on Sunday, April 29, 2007. The competition is sponsored by the College of Arts and Sciences and the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at LTU, and is one of America's longest running high school mathematics competitions. Again this year, the exam was authored by Professor Michael Merscher of LTU. Over fifty of the Detroit area's finest high school mathematics scholars took the exam. Savings bonds are awarded to the top three finishers. The winners list is as follows:

2007
First Place Sunil Agarwal 12th grade Troy High School
Second Place Seong Hyun Hwang 9th grade International Academy
Third Place Ram Bhaskar 9th grade International Academy
Honorable Mention (listed alphabetically)
